BANORANT RANKS

Ranking in BANORANT is dependent on skills, consistency, team coordination, and pressure handling abilities. As players hone their skills in aim, positioning, map knowledge, and decisions, they climb up the ladder of increasing difficulty in competition.

BE BANO

We all begin from Trash, where we commit errors, miss plays, and learn about the rules of the game. As we become better, we move up in ranks, steadily becoming better, more aware, and more threatening with each new game. The higher you rank, the more challenging the opposition gets, and those who understand the game completely will make it to the top ranks. It’s all about one thing for BANORANT: Be BANO.
