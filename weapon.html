BANORANT ARSENAL

The selection of the weapons is very crucial in every round of BANORANT as it can determine one’s approach to playing, economy, and ultimately, whether one wins or loses. There are certain types of weapons that would suit long-range shooting as opposed to those meant for short-range combat. A player must be able to identify the time to conserve money, purchase powerful rifles or even go cheap.

Phantom X

Automatic rifle that has balance and efficiency in most fight situations. Phantom X possesses excellent fire rate, controlled recoil, and efficient in close to medium range fights. It is greatly preferred by those who enjoy fast and efficient site entrances.

Vandal Core

A powerful rifle built for precision and damage. The Vandal Core is dangerous at almost any range and rewards players with accurate aim. It is perfect for players who like confident peeking, clean headshots, and high-risk, high-reward gameplay.

Spectre V9

A light submachine gun with an extremely fast fire rate. This gun does well in close combat situations and is perfect for using eco rounds or rushing. The movement provided by the gun helps pressure enemies in confined areas.

Bulldog Prime

This rifle is quite versatile and affordable, which makes it suitable for those who want good performance at low costs. It works best at medium distance and can be a good purchase from an economic standpoint for your team.

Guardian One

An assault rifle for those that have precision and control. The Guardian One can provide great damage output to whoever can control their aim. It would be best used by those who like to hold angles and shoot accurately.

Operator Z

A highly powerful sniper rifle that can eliminate your enemies with just one shot. The gun is perfect for dominating sight lines and getting rid of overconfident enemies. But positioning is important.

Sidearms

In addition to these, BANORANT also has useful pistols that are very effective for the early games and eco plays. Although sidearms are not as expensive as rifles, they can become extremely dangerous if handled by expert players.
