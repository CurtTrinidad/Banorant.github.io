BANORANT MAPS

Each map within the game, BANORANT, has a particular way of playing the game. Some maps demand speed in navigating through them, whereas others need tight corners, visibility, and control of the territory. The knowledge of the map will enable the players to make the best out of their location.

District

The District map is characterized by urban warfare, with its structured roads, connecting paths, and pressure from the middle of the map. The symmetrical nature of this map promotes collaboration, strategic rotation, and positional gameplay.

Emberfall

Emberfall is a map created from volcanoes, lava streams, fiery grounds, and high-pressure areas.The hazardous conditions encourage fierce fights and make it necessary for players to maneuver through challenging terrains.

Ruin

Ruin is an age-old jungle battleground, teeming with wild stone pathways, obscure nooks, and stacked movement avenues. This enigmatic map favors those adept at rapid adaptation, solid angles, and making the most of the terrain for unexpected plays.

Vortex

Vortex is a storm-swept battleground constructed around a powerful central element that influences the battlefield itself. The design of this map leads to dangerous choke points, flanking opportunities, and tension, which all contribute to the quick escalation of any match.

Pulse

Pulse is a high-tech map where everything runs on energy, speed, and precision. It caters to those who like to rotate rapidly, react quickly, and seize key points before the opponent gets an opportunity.
