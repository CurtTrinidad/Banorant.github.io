BANORANT AGENTS

Agents are the main components of BANORANT. Each one adds its own twist. These include attacking, defending, controlling, assisting, and gathering intelligence using a variety of methods. The selection of the agents could determine the whole fate of the game.

Blaze

Aggressiveness, intimidation, and flames are among the characteristics of the duelist named Blaze. He is very good at bringing down his opponents from standing positions while attacking first, leaving an opening for his teammates to get into the action as well.

Ciphera

Ciphera is one of the greatest sentinels because of her excellence at conducting tactical surveillance, someone who lives on information and control. With the help of her abilities, she will definitely manage to track down enemy movements and fortify their flanks.

Nova

Being one of the strongest sentinels that can provide support to her team during battles is the agent Nova. The agent can control the battle with her abilities that allow her to heal and revive teammates while she can also hold down sites with her slow effects on enemies.

Onyx

The controller agent known as onyx uses his power to create vision blocks and teleportation to control shadow movements which he uses to establish dominance on the battlefield. He excels at two tasks which include obstructing enemy sightlines and creating unpredictable situations to control his opponents' movements.

Rift

Rift is a speedy duelist that uses the power of the wind to boost his speed and precision as well as his power of producing sudden force which enhances his fighting abilities. Rift performs better when he attacks the opponent while utilizing his power of moving swiftly for dangerous fighting situations that need his best fighting abilities.

Viperon

Viperon is a controller with an ability to use venom and focuses on area denial and harassment. She is able to outperform anyone else by blocking view angles, controlling dangerous zones, and using her venomous mollys. Viperon puts pressure on enemies.

Volt

Volt is a quick electric duelist that operates based on speed and determination. He intimidates his opponents using fast assaults, daring tactics, and quick movements that ensure he exerts continuous pressure against his enemies on the battlegrounds.
